### Full Description (4000 chars max)
Masjidly brings your mosque community together in one beautiful, easy-to-use app.

STAY CONNECTED TO YOUR MOSQUE
Follow any mosque and receive real-time notifications for prayer times, upcoming events, announcements, and Friday Khutbah reminders. Whether you're a long-time member or new to the area, Masjidly helps you stay informed and engaged.

FIND MOSQUES NEAR YOU
Discover mosques in your city using your location. View their prayer schedules, upcoming events, videos, and community posts — all without needing an account.

PRAYER TIMES
Access daily Adhan and Iqamah times for Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, and Isha. Mosques publish their exact schedules so you always know when to pray. Jumu'ah and Taraweeh schedules are also available.

EVENTS & RSVPs
Browse and RSVP to upcoming events at your mosque — halaqa circles, Eid prayers, fundraisers, community dinners, and more. Get reminders so you never miss what matters.

ANNOUNCEMENTS & COMMUNITY POSTS
Read mosque announcements, like and comment on posts, and engage with your community. Urgent and important posts are highlighted so critical information always reaches you.

VIDEOS & LECTURES
Watch Friday Khutbahs, Islamic lectures, and community videos directly in the app with a beautiful full-screen player.

COMMUNITY POLLS
Participate in mosque polls and help your leadership make better decisions for the community.

DIRECT MESSAGING
Send messages directly to your mosque administration with questions or requests.

FOR MOSQUE ADMINISTRATORS
Masjidly includes a full-featured admin panel for mosque managers:
• Post announcements and pin important messages
• Create and manage events with RSVP tracking
• Publish and update daily prayer times
• Upload videos and documents
• Manage your team with role-based access
• View follower analytics and engagement
• Moderate content reports
• Run community polls

PRIVACY FIRST
Masjidly does not sell your data. Location is only used to find nearby mosques and is never stored on our servers.

Download Masjidly and strengthen your connection to your mosque community today.
